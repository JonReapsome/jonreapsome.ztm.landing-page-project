The art of photography.

At Jonathan Andrew Photography, capturing moments in time to be displayed for years to come is something that isn't taken lightly. Photos have the power to capture and evoke complex emotions. Nostalgia, joyous memories, and hope for the future are just a few things that can be captured in a singular image. What part of your story do you want to stop in time and hold onto going forward?

It kind of sounds like a lot of pressure, right? Well, yes and no. The beautiful thing about digital photography is that it gives us the power to capture many, many images in a short amount of time. And often times it is the shot that wasn't quite as calculated or rehearsed that ends up being the one that captures the moment better than all the rest.

That said, when creating a time and space for this to happen, there is a lot to consider. What we do at Jonathan Andrew Photography is we take care of that part for our clients so that all they have to do is provide us with the subject, whether that be themselves, their business or property, or their product(s).
